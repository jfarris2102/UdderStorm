Although the local copy of the game works fine, the most up to date version can be found here:

http://people.ucsc.edu/~jpfarris/GetYourAsstoMars/index.html


For graders, press 'H' (for hacks). This will add money and research points, unlock buildings,
and increase the Terraforming progress on Mars.


The Goal of our game in short is to build up a colony on Mars in hopes of Terraforming
the planet before the Earth dies out due to overpopulation, polution, and such.
